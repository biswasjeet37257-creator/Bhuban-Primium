Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Bhuban collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, profile picture
  • Content Data: Videos, comments, likes, and uploads
  • Usage Data: Watch history, search queries, device information
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Preferences: Video quality settings, notification preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our video streaming services
  • Personalize your content recommendations
  • Process video uploads and transcoding (HD, 2K, 4K)
  • Communicate with you about updates and features
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption:

  • Local Storage: Preferences and settings stored on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Videos and user data encrypted at rest
  • Transmission: All data transmitted over HTTPS
  • Backups: Regular automated backups to prevent data loss

4. Video Quality & Processing

When you upload videos, we process them in multiple qualities:

  • 4K (2160p) - Ultra High Definition
  • 2K (1440p) - Quad HD
  • 1080p - Full HD
  • 720p - HD
  • 480p - Standard Definition

Your original upload is preserved, and you control which quality is used for playback.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login status and preferences
  • Track video playback progress
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Provide personalized recommendations

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Video hosting and CDN delivery
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Payment processing (for premium features)
  • AI-powered content recommendations

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your uploaded content
  • Opt-out: Disable personalized recommendations

8. Children's Privacy

Bhuban is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can delete your account and data at any time through Settings.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@bhuban.com
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@bhuban.com
